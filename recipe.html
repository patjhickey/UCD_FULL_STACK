Steak & Chips

Ingredients

  1. 2 x 8Oz Fillet Steak
  2. 4 x Medium Size Potatoes
  3. 1 Large Onion
  4. 10 Oz Button Mushrooms

Preparation and Cooking


  • Wash the potatoes in cold water and peel and slice
  • Put the chips in hot oi and cook for 10 minutes
  • Sear the steak in a hot pan and place in the oven at 200 degrees C for 8 mins (medium rare), 10 mins (medium) and 15 mins for well done.
  • Saute the onions and mushrooms in a hot frying pan with 4Oz of salted butter)

Serve with a nice Rioja - Faustino No 1.
